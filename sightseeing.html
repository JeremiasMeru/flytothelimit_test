AWESOME SIGHT SEEING FLIGHTS

Operating out of Queenstown and Wanaka, FTTL offer tourists a range of scenic flights in both fixed wing and helicopters, as well as high adventure in a stunt plane, or the most peaceful gliding flight experience possible in a glider high above the mountains. They specialize in one-off charters, and delivery and pickup for trampers, also providing a provisioning service for fresh supplies during long excursions.
